Bio

  • I am now a 4th-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I received my B.S. degree from the IEEE Honor Class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, in 2017. I am quite fortunate to be advised by Prof. Fan Wu. I also feel very happy to work closely with Prof. Shaojie Tang, Prof. Zhenzhe Zheng, and Prof. Guihai Chen.
  • My research interests include data sharing (e.g., federated learning and data trading), deep learning (e.g., recommender systems and distributed optimization), as well as security and privacy (e.g., privacy preservation and verifiable computation). My long-term research goal is to facilitate ultra-scale data shared in an intelligent, secure, and economically-robust way.
  • I ever held internships in Alibaba Group from Nov. 2018 to Feb. 2020 and in Intel Asia-Pacific R&D Ltd. from Nov. 2016 to Aug. 2017.
